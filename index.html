About MatchYard

We're building the home for local sport.

MatchYard began with a simple frustration: wanting to play, and having nowhere to turn. We exist to make an active life easier to share — one match, run and pickup game at a time.

Our story

Sport is the fastest way to feel at home somewhere.

Move to a new city and the court, the trail and the gym are where you meet people — if you can find someone to go with. We built MatchYard so that finding a partner or a game is never the reason you skip it.

Every decision we make comes back to one question: does this get more people moving, together, more often?

What we believe

Four principles we don't compromise on.

Activity-first
You're here to play, not to be marketed to or matched romantically.
Local by design
The best game is the one a few minutes away. We build for your neighborhood.
Trust by default
Real profiles, clear reputations and privacy you control — from the first tap.
For every level
Beginners belong here as much as regulars. Everyone starts somewhere.

Come build the movement with us.

We're just getting started — and we'd love for you to be early.
