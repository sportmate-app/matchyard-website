How it works

From signup to first serve in three steps.

STEP 01

Build your sports profile

Pick your sports, set your skill for each, and mark the times you're usually free to play.

STEP 02

Swipe to match

Browse nearby players ranked by fit. Swipe right on anyone you'd like to play with — match when you both do.

STEP 03

Chat & play

Message your match, lock in a time and a court, and get on with the game.

Phone verification, photo review, and easy reporting and blocking come standard. We always nudge you to meet in public places. That said, meeting people you don't know carries real-world risk — take sensible precautions, tell a friend where you're going, and trust your instincts.

You can delete your account at any time, directly in the app. Go to Profile → Delete Account. Deletion is permanent and removes your profile, posts, chats, messages, and ratings.

Tap the ⋯ menu on a profile, post, or chat and choose Report. You can also block a user from the same menu. We have a zero-tolerance policy for objectionable content and abusive users, and we act on reports — typically within 24 hours.
